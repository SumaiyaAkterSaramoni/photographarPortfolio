Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my photography services

How far in advance should I book a photography session? +

For portrait sessions, I recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance. For weddings and events, it's best to book 6-12 months ahead to secure your date. However, I sometimes have last-minute availability, so feel free to contact me anytime.

What should we wear for our photo session? +

I recommend wearing clothing that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Solid colors and subtle patterns generally photograph best. For group photos, coordinating colors (but not matching outfits) creates a cohesive look. I provide a detailed style guide to all clients before their session.

How long does it take to receive our photos? +

Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks. Weddings and events take 4-6 weeks due to the larger volume of images. You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours of your session!

Do you travel for photo sessions? +

Yes! I'm available for travel worldwide. Local travel within 50 miles of my studio is included in all packages. For destinations beyond that, I charge a reasonable travel fee based on location and duration.
